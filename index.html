About the Property

Welcome to Foresthill Lakópark, where modern living meets natural tranquility. This exceptional ground floor apartment (Unit 3A.F.1 in Building F/D) offers direct access to a spacious private garden. With a total area of 99.49 m², this thoughtfully designed home features 2 bedrooms, an open-plan living area, and generous outdoor spaces including two terraces and a private garden of over 40 m².

The apartment is located in the prestigious Foresthill Lakópark development at Pusztakúti út, offering a perfect balance of peaceful suburban living with easy access to Budapest city center. The property includes a dedicated parking space (P54 - 13.42 m²), ensuring convenience for residents.
